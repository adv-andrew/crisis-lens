Diagram showing humanitarian funding flow and the gap analysis.

Show a top-to-bottom flow:

1. Donor countries pool funds into two channels:
   - Country-Based Pooled Funds (CBPF) — flexible, local allocation
   - Bilateral aid — direct government-to-government transfers

2. OCHA FTS tracks both channels and publishes requirements vs. actuals.

3. CrisisLens computes the Funding Gap = (Required - Received) / Required for each country-year.

4. The Funding Gap feeds into the OCI score alongside Severity and Media Neglect.

5. Output: ranked list of most underfunded crises, funding forecast with confidence intervals, reallocation simulator.
